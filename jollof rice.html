Jollof Rice

Description

It is a one-pot rice dish and is of great historical significance.The dish goes back to as long ago as 1500s

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice
  • 5-6 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 yellow chilli pepper/ bonnet peppers (optional - only if you can find it)
  • cups chicken stock/vegetable stock/water
  • tsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp thyme/basil leaves/oregano/any other herb
  • Salt to taste
  • Half tbsp chopped garlic
  • 1 tsp chopped ginger
  • 2 bay leaves (tej patta)

Steps

  1. Rinse rice and boil it in water with some salt till almost cooked. Alternatively, you can cook rise with tomato puree also
  2. Heat oil/ghee in a pan. Roast bay leaf, ginger, garlic, chilli pepper and onions.
  3. Let the mixture cool down and grind it.
  4. In the same pan, add back onion and spice mixture, add capsicum, tomato puree , some salt, curry water and stock/water, and let the paste boil for 10-15 minutes till gravy thickens and tomatoes are cooked.
  5. Add boiled rice, mix well and cook for another 5 minutes.
  6. Turn off the gas, garnish with herbs and serve.
