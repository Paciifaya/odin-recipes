Yam And Egg

Description

Boiled yam and fried eggs is a common breakfast choice in Nigerian homes.

Ingredients

  • 10 pieces of yam (chopped)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 small size tomato
  • 1 scotch bonnet pepper (ata rodo)
  • Handfull chopped spring onions
  • Handful chopped carrot
  • 1 seasoning cube
  • 1/4 teaspoon curry
  • A pinch of thyme
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. In a pot of boiling water, peel and chop your yam and bring to boil.
  2. when yam is soft, season with salt and drain the water.
  3. In a pan, heat up the oil and stir fry the onions and chopped pepper.
  4. Add the tomato and carrots after a minute of frying the onion and pepper on medium heat.
  5. Add the seasoning and stir fry for another minute.
  6. In a bowl, whisk your eggs and pour into the frying vegetables.
  7. Reduce the heat and allow the eggs cook while stirring it.
  8. Increase the heat after a minute and properly stir fry the egg and serve with the yam.
